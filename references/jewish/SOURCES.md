## lulav_etrog.jpg
Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Arbaat_haminim-new.jpg
Direct URL: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/10/Arbaat_haminim-new.jpg
License: CC BY-SA 4.0 International (attribution + share-alike)
Notes: All four species laid out on a wooden surface: aravah (willow, left), lulav (palm, vertical center, in woven holder), hadass (myrtle, right), etrog (citrus fruit, bottom right). Etrog is greenish (some etrogim are yellow at full ripeness; this one is on the green side) but unmistakably an etrog with the characteristic bumpy oval shape.
