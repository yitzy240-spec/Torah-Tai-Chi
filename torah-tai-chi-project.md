# Torah Tai Chi Project Overview

## Project Summary

Torah Tai Chi is a wellness influencer/blogger brand that merges Jewish Torah wisdom with tai chi-inspired mindful movement practices. The project involves building a complete digital presence including a website and social media channels, with content delivered through an animated character guide.

---

## Client Information

- **Client:** Yonah Lloyd
- **Developer/Consultant:** Yitzy Marcus
- **Project Start:** January 2025
- **Status:** In Development

---

## Brand Identity

### Name
**Torah Tai Chi**

### Concept
A unique wellness brand that bridges two ancient wisdom traditions—Jewish Torah teachings and tai chi movement philosophy—through modern, accessible content.

### Brand Character
An animated character guide who serves as the consistent face of the brand across all video content, making spiritual concepts approachable and memorable.

### Tone of Voice
- Warm / Friendly
- Inspirational

### Aesthetic
Calm, welcoming, and contemporary—appealing to both observant Jews and those curious about Jewish wisdom traditions.

## Content Strategy

### Content Types
- **Daily wisdom snippets** – Short, bite-sized Torah insights
- **Guided movement practices** – Tai chi-inspired exercises rooted in Jewish teachings
- **Reflective content** – Connecting physical health to spiritual growth
- **Blog posts** – Deeper explorations of themes
- **Video clips** – Featuring the animated character guide

### Content Delivery
Short, engaging video clips delivered through the animated character guide across all social platforms, with the website serving as the central hub.

## Website Structure

### Planned Pages/Sections
1. **Home** – Brand introduction and featured content
2. **About Us** – Mission statement and animated guide character introduction
3. **Video Content** – Embedded videos from social platforms
4. **Blog** – Written content and deeper explorations
5. **Social Media Links** – Integration with all platforms
6. **Newsletter Signup** – Email list building

### Site Goals
1. Build brand awareness and establish Torah Tai Chi as a recognized voice in Jewish wellness
2. Drive traffic to social media channels and grow following

4. Build an email list through newsletter signups
5. Establish credibility before expanding into future offerings (courses, merchandise)
